About

What does a software engineer's diet look like? Lots of bugs. Before becoming a software engineer, I worked as a lab technician testing for food pathogens. During my free time at the time, I was exploring coding for fun and quickly decided I wanted to pivot my career into engineering.

I am a graduate of App Academy 24 weeks full time software engineering program. During the program, I learned JavaScript, React, Express, Python, and many more technologies for web development. Combined with experiance and skills from my previous jobs, I bring a solid skillset to a collaborative workspace as a well-rounded software engineer!

When I'm not coding, I enjoy going out and about trying new food and restaurants. I have a lot of fun experimenting with cooking although I'm not the best cook. I also really enjoy playing a large variety of video games late into the night.
